# Pixel Chomp Polish and QA Skill

## Purpose
Push Pixel Chomp from functional to satisfying.

## Areas of focus
- control feel
- readability
- pacing
- audio feedback
- visual juice
- bug prevention

## Feel targets
- buffered turns should feel reliable
- pellet pickup should feel crisp
- enemy contact should be unambiguous
- death and respawn downtime should stay short
- menus should be fast and readable

## Juice ideas
- tiny scale pop on pellet pickup
- screen flash or pulse on power activation
- score popup for enemy chains
- subtle camera nudge on death
- animated HUD when high score changes

## QA scenarios
- turning at every type of intersection
- pellet clear edge cases
- enemy frightened expiration during overlap
- respawn resets
- pause/unpause consistency
- local high score persistence

## Release bar
Do not call it playable until:
- no blocker bugs
- movement feels intentional
- at least one full game session can be completed without confusion
